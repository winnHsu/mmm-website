MetroMesh Media

America’s biggest delivery-based media network

MetroMesh brings brands into the city’s hardest-to-reach spaces by transforming delivery riders into high-impact, hyper-local advertising.

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Manhattan.

MetroMesh Media is a next-generation OOH media company built for dense, fast-moving cities. Generating millions of impressions every single day, we operate the largest delivery-powered advertising network in the U.S., giving brands access to streets, sidewalks, residential buildings, and commercial interiors.

  • For Riders

    We create a reliable, passive income stream for delivery riders, adding meaningful earnings without extra effort, added costs, or changes to their daily routes.

  • For Cities

    We move responsibly through urban environments, reaching local communities without visual clutter while supporting public initiatives and city messaging at street level.

  • For Brands

    We place brands in high-frequency, high-intent environments, combining the reach of out-of-home with the precision and measurability of digital media.

  1. Built with People

    We design our network around the people who power it—riders, partners, brands, and the communities they move through every day.

  2. Operational approach

    Every product we launch solves a real-world problem. Our adtech is built for cities, tested in motion, and optimized where it actually performs.

  3. Service quality

    Visibility is a guarantee. From uptime to execution, we deliver consistent, high-quality campaigns brands can trust—ride after ride.

Our focus is on turning complex urban environments into meaningful media opportunities.
